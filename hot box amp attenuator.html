Output network. R11 = series top rail (after R8). Out shunt = parallel across speaker. Out return = series in speaker– return. For Hewitt 16 Ω mod, set R11=10, R10=68 in Stage 1.
Network 4-stage L-pad ladder
Stage 1 morphs with frequency: at LF (L1 ≈ short) R1 sits at the input; at HF (L1 ≈ open) R2B+R1 sit as a shunt at the output. Roughly −7 dB at both extremes when loaded by the speaker.
Stages 2–4: SPDT wiper sits on the top rail before each R-series. Engaged ⇒ wiper down-left to the R-shunt path (R3/R5/R7 to GND). Bypass ⇒ wiper down-right to the wire shorting R-series. The R-shunt floats in bypass.
Outputs: Out1/Out2 (TS) direct-tap for 8 Ω cabs (or two 16 Ω in parallel). Out3 (TRS) uses R11 in series and R10 from node M for a single 16 Ω cab. In bypass, the jumper also taps Out3 Tip directly, shunting R11.
